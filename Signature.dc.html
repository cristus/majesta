Inspired by African heritage and the rarest of stones, MAJESTA is a study in patience — gemstones gathered over years, set entirely by hand, composed into pieces that speak softly and last forever.

A Coming-Soon Edition
Heritage in Every Setting

The story of MAJESTA begins not in a boutique but in the earth — in the emerald seams and sapphire deposits that have made the continent a quiet capital of colour for centuries. Each stone is chosen for its fire, its clarity, its character.

Our ateliers work in the old manner: by hand, by eye, by feel. A single necklace may pass through a dozen pairs of hands before it is ready. We do not rush what is meant to endure.

What arrives this autumn is not a catalogue but a point of view — adornment as inheritance, beauty composed to be passed down rather than worn out.

“Every stone carries the light of the continent that gave it.”

The peacock — emblem of ceremony and colour across the continent — guides the palette of the house: emerald greens, sapphire blues, the warm gold of the savannah.
